# Wavelet analysis of energy transfer paths

This paper is devoted to the wavelet analysis of energy transfer paths in a quasi-geostrophic system with zonal jets

## 1. Problem Statement

Consider the potential vorticity equation in the quasi-geostrophic approximation:

$$\begin{equation}
\frac{\mathrm{D}q}{\mathrm{D} t} = - r q + \nu \nabla^2 q + f
\end{equation}
$$

where $\mathrm{D}q/\mathrm{D} t$ is the Lagrange derivative, r is the coefficient of friction, $\nu$ is the viscosity, and f is the forcing.

## 2. Dissipative-free case

$$\begin{equation}
\frac{\mathrm{D}q}{\mathrm{D} t} = 0
\end{equation}$$
